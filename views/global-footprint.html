Our Global Footprint

Worldwide Distribution

SKMEI watches are distributed and enjoyed by customers in over 160 countries and regions worldwide. Our extensive network of partners and distributors ensures that quality SKMEI timepieces are accessible to a global audience. We are continuously expanding our reach to serve more communities and bring our innovative products to every corner of the world.

Our Partners

We collaborate with a vast network of international partners, including authorized dealers, online retailers, and regional distributors. These partnerships are crucial to our global success, allowing us to maintain high standards of service and product availability across diverse markets.

  • Strong relationships with over 500 distributors globally.
  • Presence in major e-commerce platforms worldwide.
  • Dedicated support for our international partners.

Key Markets

Our key markets span across Asia, Europe, North America, South America, Africa, and Oceania. We adapt our strategies to meet the unique demands and preferences of each region, ensuring cultural relevance and market penetration.

  • Asia: China, India, Southeast Asia, Middle East
  • Europe: Russia, Germany, France, UK
  • Americas: USA, Brazil, Mexico
  • Africa & Oceania: South Africa, Australia
